Today's Recipe

Today, I want to share with you a delicious recipe that I recently discovered. This Creamy Tomato Basil Pasta is a crowd-pleaser!

Ingredients

  • 300g pasta
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes
  • 1 cup heavy cream

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions.
  2. In a skillet, cook cherry tomatoes until they burst.
  3. Add heavy cream and cooked pasta to the skillet. Stir until well combined.
